How to Make Salad


This is the tutorial of a salad. There are numerous forms of salads as they are customizable. This salad will follow a more basic and barebone recipe.


Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Cucumber
  • Shredded Carrots
  • Walnuts
  • Croutons
  • Dressing of Choice

Steps

  1. Collect bowl and big utencil
  2. Chop cucumber and walnuts if needed
  3. Pour 1/2 bag of spinach in bowl
  4. Pour 3 chopped cucumbers in bowl
  5. Pour 1/2 cup of shredded carrots
  6. Pour 1/2 cup of walnuts
  7. Mix ingredients
  8. Pour dressing
  9. Mix again
  10. Top with croutons
  11. Serve and enjoy!
