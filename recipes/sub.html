How to Make a Sub


This tutorial follows a simple classic. This variation will be vegetarian but feel free to add any ingredients of your choice!

Inredients

  • French Bread
  • Chopped Lettuce
  • Spinach
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Sliced Provalone

Steps

  1. Chop tomato and cucumber
  2. Open bread and lay all ingredients
  3. --optional--Toss open sub into oven at 350 for 6 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
