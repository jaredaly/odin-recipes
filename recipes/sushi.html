How to Make Sushi


This recipe is a bit more unique and will involve more crafting!


Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Seaweed
  • Cucumbers
  • Carrots
  • Avocado

Steps -assuming your rice is cooked

  1. Chop cucumbers, carrots, and avocados
  2. Spread rice on flat piece of seaweed
  3. Lay cucumbers, carrots, and avocados out in rows
  4. Neatly roll sushi from the bottom up
  5. Slice sushi and enjoy!
